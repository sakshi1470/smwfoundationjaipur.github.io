Education

Mission--Vission--Solution

Mission

Our E-Learning program aims at providing quality education to the underprivileged section and rural part of our country.

The Problem

The schools providing good education are unaffordable by the underprivileged ones due to high fees charged by them. Villages too are deprived of good schools.
It has been noticed a very large number of students drop out at the primary education stage due to lack of motivation towards study and lack of knowledge of opportunities.

The reason behind this is- a large number of schools don’t have proper infrastructure, they even lack good teachers, and thus don’t have quality teaching methodologies.

Solution

Our E-Learning Programme is an answer to this issue. Our target is to provide the school with quality and interactive e-modules. The complete syllabus of standard 1st to 10th will be made to audio-visual interactive formats by the e-learning experts who will make learning a fun-filled experience for the children.
Secondly, hardware such as Smart TV, Pen drives, memory chips, speakers, CPU, mouse, projectors, hard disks will be provided to the school for running e-modules.
Thirdly, the teachers will be properly trained to use the hardware and the e-modules. On-site assistance will also be provided as per the requirement.
Lastly, the school's progress will be properly monitored by us
