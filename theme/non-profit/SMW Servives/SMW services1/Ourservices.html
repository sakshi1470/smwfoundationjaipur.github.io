Our Program

Education

SMW Foundation aims at providing primary education to those children who are deprived of it due to their inability to pay fees. It aims at providing them with proper skills and knowledge so that they can have a bright future instead of working as child labour for a few bucks. Our Foundation believes in Education for all, and thus also work for educating women and old age people who are interested in it.

Health Education


As precaution is always better than cure, we provide health education to the uneducated and underprivileged ones, so that they can apply it in their daily lifestyle and hence maintain their proper hygiene, resulting in their better health.Empowering behavior change and actions through increased knowledge.

Women Safety

India has developed very fast in these years, but still a large section of our women are unsafe, deprived of basic respect, even at their home. Our main objective is to empower women by making them skillful so that they can earn their bread and protect themselves. Thus giving them enough strength to raise their voice against any kind of violence(domestic/ non-domestic) against them.

Save Plants, Save the Nature

This program focuses on Environment & Human Well-Being, by contributing towards preservation and restoration of the environment, thus creating a sustainable future. It aims at educating the people about the things harming our environment and how to control them. The program also focuses on tree plantation and developing the tree plantation skills in youth and children.

One Step Towards Cleanliness.

This program aims at contributing towards the most popular campaign that Modi Government launched:- Swach Bharat Abhiyan. It looks forward to ending open defecation and improve waste management.

Khoob Khelo Khub Bado

As we know children are future of our nation, this program aims at teaching human value, good habit, civic sense, emotional intelligence to them through play and educational toys. Qualified and trained teachers will help underprivileged children in improving their learning ability and social skill through recreational activities.

Skill Learning and Skill development

Skill development is the process of identifying our skill gaps and developing and honing these skills. skills determine our ability to execute our plans with sucess.
